FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

A selection of questions we’re often asked. Have suggestions? Ping us!

What cameras can I use?

Any consumer, semi-professional or professional camera will do the trick.

Just make sure the device can deliver the FPS and resolution rates recommended for your circumstances.

What frame rate per second (fps) do you recommend?

We recommend no less than 24 fps.

For motion that is fast to the human eye (e.g. dance, action, sports, etc.), increase your fps rate as much as possible.

On most smartphones you should select 120 fps (available in slo-mo mode).

Can I choose any resolution?

We generally recommend uploading content at 1080p HD.

While 720p HD will work for most content, lower resolutions may result in a loss of accuracy, depending on the content you’re processing.

Can I shoot indoors and outdoors?

Yes absolutely You can shoot indoors, outdoors

Should the actor be wearing specific clothing?

No specific clothing is required. To ensure maximum accuracy in detecting the human body, we don’t recommend baggy clothing that obscures too much of the body. In low contrast or low lighting settings, we also recommend high contrast clothing for best results.

Are there any restrictions around the distance between the actor and the camera?

There are no specific physical distance (or proximity) requirements.

For best results, we recommend, that the actor takes up out no less than 35% of the length of the frame. At large distances, a strong zoom may well do the trick.
