GUIDE

The RAD platform was developed so it can be used by anyone at any time. We can process almost anything.

But to get outstanding results as fast as possible, we provide guidance below. Enjoy!

FIXED CAMERA / LENS Avoid panning, zooming and moving.

Stabilize the camera by holding it steady or mounting it on a tripod. Avoid panning, zooming or moving the camera during the shoot.

While our AI will still detect pose and motion, moving the camera, zooming or panning may negatively affect relative depth perception in your results.

24/30 120/240
fps
FRAME RATE Higher frame rates are better.

Generally, we recommend a minimum of 24 frames per second (fps).

For motion that is fast to the human eye (e.g. dance, action, sports, etc.), increase the fps rate to no less than 120, going up to 240 fps (and beyond) for extremely fast and complex motion.

1080pi
RESOLUTION Higher resolution is better.

We recommend videos with a resolution of no less than 1080p.

Lower resolutions may result in a loss of accuracy, depending on the content you’re processing.

COMPRESSION Lower compression is better.
